The Tiger

Tigers are the largest and most powerful of all the cats they are 10 times stronger than a man! They live all over Asia from the rainforest to the woodlands of Siberia.

A baby tiger is called a cub. These cubs are as tiny as newborn kittens. Their eyes and ears are still closed. After 6 months older the cubs follows their mother everywhere. She shows them how to hunt for food.

Their stripes and colors help them to hide in tall grass. They stay very quiet when they hunt. They walk very slowly. Then suddenly they pounce!
